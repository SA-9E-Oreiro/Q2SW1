Characters Info Chibi Form

Dr. Veritas Ratio, a blunt and intelligent young man, who often covers his face with a unique plaster sculpture.He has shown incredible display of talent and intellect since his youth which rewarded him many praises and even a spot in the Intelligentsia Guild.

Aventurine, real name Kakavasha, is a senior manager in the IPC Strategic Investment Department and one of the Ten Stonehearts. He was blessed by Gaiathra Triclops to have good luck for the rest of his life. Due to his luck, he won his current position as one of the Ten Stonehearts by gambling against fate itself.

Boothill, a cyborg cowboy traveling within the galaxy. He is extremely optimistic and loose. A man who doesn’t hesitate freeing his revolver from his holster when he feels a sense of danger. He is a member of the Galaxy Rangers, a faction who has a strong code of punishing those who commit injustice.

Argenti, a model knight of the faction Knights of Beauty. This righteous noble who cruises around the cosmos alone. He is a firm believer in the path of beauty and his main goal is to spread the name of his god, Idrila.

Sunday, the head of the Oak Family in Penacony. A young man who has a strong belief that the weak will always be devoured, therefore the strong should govern and protect the weak. He took this ideology to the extreme and planned to revive a fallen god to keep the people in a dream, so they don’t need to suffer in the real world. Currently, he is finding his own path after his plan failed and he is trying to achieve what freedom is.
